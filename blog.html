Prosperity

On occasion a herd of sheep are able to congregate and slither by a pack of wolves from a twilight snack. This deathly routine carries on night by night as it is the center of their universe. The survival to see another 24 for the prey and the battle against starvation for the predator. What they both lack though, is the comprehension of the real predator at play. It’s me. It’s me that takes the wool off a sheep’s back. It’s me that slays a sheep that doesn’t produce. And It’s me that slays the wolf to protect my sheep. Not because I care for the sheep but because the sheep is my slave and the wolf is my puppet. The sheep doesn’t bite the hand that feeds them and the wolf does not fear what he does not know. I am the eyes, ears, and dagger to their world. But I am no lone wolf. I’m just the human with no pack.
